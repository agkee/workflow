## Notes for the facilitator

- Keep the group on one topic at a time.
- If the conversation drifts, move it to the parking lot instead of letting it contaminate the main notes.
- At the end of each topic, ask for a 30-second recap of the decision, task, and blocker.
- Make sure every task candidate has an intended outcome, not just an activity.
